353x500 Mercedes F1 WO9 EQ Power+

This is a picture of the 2018 Mercedes Formula One car. There are many new things with the Formula One cars this year. The most noteable change between last years car and this years is the "Halo" that is surrounding the cockpit. This is a new saftey measure that was implemented to further protect drivers in the event of the car crashing and flipping.
